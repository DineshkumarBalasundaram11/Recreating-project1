About Us

Inspiring youth around the world

At our NPO, we are a team of passionate individuals who are committed to creating a positive impact on the world. We believe that businesses have a responsibility to not only focus on profits, but also consider their impact on the environment, society, and governance practices. By raising awareness about ESG, we aim to encourage young individuals and organizations to adopt sustainable and responsible practices that benefit people, the planet, and long-term economic growth.

Our Story

In a world where ESG principles were reshaping business and society, we, Aditya Balaji and Sreevatsen, two teenagers driven by our passion for change, couldn't stand on the sidelines. We saw the transformative power of ESG and its growing importance. This realization led to the creation of ESG Zoomers, our non-profit organization.


ESG Zoomers is our vehicle for inspiring and educating youth about ESG's significance. We understood that this knowledge was crucial for the next generation to make informed decisions, advocate for responsible practices, and shape a more sustainable future. Through ESG Zoomers, we engage in interviews with CEOs and business leaders at the forefront of ESG adoption. These conversations reveal that ESG is not just a business strategy; it's a commitment to the well-being of our world and its communities. Our journey is one of curiosity, dedication, and a deep-seated desire to drive positive change, one interview, one young mind at a time. Join us as we explore ESG's transformative potential and collectively shape a more sustainable and responsible future.
